    Abstract:
    Obesity is one of the fastest-developing public health problems. The general population needs to have a balanced and healthy diet as it provides the energy and nutrients required to survive and to stay healthy. Combining a healthy diet with an active lifestyle has huge health benefits and helps reducing the risk of major health problems such as heart disease, cancer, and obesity. With the help of experienced dietitians, our diet system will provide an easy and a smart way to deliver healthy food and the appropriate diet plan to our clients.
